About Us

Welcome to Khushiyaan Foundation, where joy meets purpose! Established with a heartfelt commitment to spreading happiness and making a meaningful impact, Khushiyaan Foundation is a beacon of hope and positivity. Our foundation is driven by the belief that every individual deserves access to essential resources, education, and opportunities to lead a fulfilling life. With a dedicated team and a network of compassionate volunteers, we strive to create a ripple effect of positive change in communities facing various challenges. From education and healthcare initiatives to sustainable development projects, Khushiyaan Foundation is actively working towards building a brighter and more equitable future for all. Join us in our journey to bring smiles to faces, empower lives, and foster a sense of joy and well-being in every corner of our society. Together, let's create a world where kindness and happiness thrive, leaving a lasting impact on generations to come. Welcome to Khushiyaan Foundation - where we believe in spreading joy, one meaningful act at a time.

Our Programs

Clothes donation Program

A heartfelt initiative by Khushiyaan foundation to extend warmth and care to those in need. we understand the transformative power of clothing, and we invite you to join us in making a difference. Your gently used or new clothing donations can become a source of comfort and confidence for individuals facing adversity. Whether it's a warm jacket for the winter or professional attire to empower someone entering the workforce, your contribution matters. By participating in our Clothes Donation Program, you are not just giving away garments; you are providing dignity and a sense of belonging to those less fortunate. Together, let's weave a tapestry of compassion and clothing, creating a community that cares for one another. Join us in dressing the world with kindness and making a tangible impact on the lives of those who need it most.

Hunger Alleviation Program

Welcome to Khushiyaan Foundation's Food Donation Program, where generosity nourishes the soul! In our commitment to alleviating hunger and fostering community well-being, we invite you to be a part of this impactful initiative. By donating non-perishable food items or contributing to our fundraising efforts, you become a vital link in the chain of compassion that feeds those facing food insecurity. Every canned good, staple, or monetary donation paves the way for a healthier and more secure future for individuals and families in need. Join us in transforming the simple act of giving into a profound gesture of support, as we strive to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry. Together, let's share the abundance and cultivate a community where nourishment is a right, not a privilege. Join Khushiyaan Foundation in the fight against hunger, because no one should face an empty plate.
